Frequently asked questions

What counts as a "conversation"?

One conversation is a complete interaction — e.g. a full phone call, or a chat session from first message to resolution. We count unique sessions, not individual messages.

How long does setup take?

Most clients are live within 10–14 business days. Complex enterprise integrations may take up to 4 weeks depending on your tech stack.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a free 30-minute strategy call and a pilot scoping session. Paid pilots can be arranged for Enterprise clients.

Do you offer GDPR-compliant solutions?

Yes. All data is processed within the EU. We sign a DPA (Data Processing Agreement) with every client. On-premise deployment is available on Enterprise plans.
